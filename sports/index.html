Beep Test by Sport

Position-specific benchmarks, what elite players score and how to train for your sport.

The beep test is used across team sports as a standard aerobic fitness benchmark — but what counts as a good score varies enormously depending on your sport and position. A level 10 is outstanding for a front-row forward and merely average for an elite AFL midfielder. Context matters.

Each page below covers the typical score ranges for that sport at different levels of competition, why the demands vary by position, how professional athletes compare and how to structure your preparation.
