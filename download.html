How to Use the Audio File

Download the file to your phone before your training session — do not rely on a mobile data connection during the test. Gaps in audio due to buffering will invalidate your result.

Play the file through a speaker loud enough for all participants to hear clearly. In a large sports hall, a portable bluetooth speaker works well. If any participant cannot hear the beeps, the test is not valid for them.

The audio includes a spoken level and shuttle announcement before each new level begins. Listen for the double beep — it signals a level change and a slight increase in pace.
